What IS Second Hand International National?

We are an online retailer dedicated to connect users to vendors of foreign goods without the costs of international shipping.

Why cater to a second hand foreign goods market?

Recent trading policies have increased the price of goods for consumers and sellers of foreign goods. By offering a dedicated place to connect local sellers, we can offer a wide selection of foreign goods while avoiding higher prices.
